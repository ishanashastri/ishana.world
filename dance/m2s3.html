MENACE II SOCIETY VOL. 3 [2024]

I led, choreographed, and staged this piece as part of my dance team's annual show, Mocha Show: Mo'Yearbook 2024. Featuring Kryptonite by Big Boi, Rock-D, Blackwned C-Bone, and Killer Mike, this piece is the third edition of Menace II Society, following Vol 1 and Vol 2. As per usual, it focuses on getting hype and being bigheaded with your squad. However, the intention for this edition was to only feature one song and focus more on cohesive group dynamics and flow.

Special thanks to my dancers for bringing the hype and energy.
