MENACE II SOCIETY [2022]

I led, choreographed, and staged this piece as part of my dance team's annual show, Mocha Show: Mo'Mixtape 2022. Featuring Big Black Truck by JID + Dreamville, Insomniak by Mac Miller, Ghetto Symphony by A$AP Rocky, and I Ain't Got Time by Tyler the Creator, this piece is the original rendition of Menace II Society Vol. 2 and Menace II Society Vol. 3. It focuses on getting hype and being bigheaded with your squad.

This was the first choreography I creatively directed and put together. Special thanks to Jade Ishii and Sophia Chen for helping to choreograph and to my dancers for putting their bodies to the test.
