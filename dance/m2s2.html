MENACE II SOCIETY VOL. 2 [2023]

I led, choreographed, and staged this piece as part of my dance team's annual show, Mocha Show: Mo'Flix 2023. Featuring Learn How to Watch by Carnage + Mac Miller, Superhero by Metro Boomin + Future, and Where My Dogs At? by A$AP Ferg, this piece is the second edition of Menace II Society. It focuses on getting hype and being bigheaded with your squad.

Special thanks to Kevin Sun Chen for choreographing the second song and to my dancers for bringing the hype.
