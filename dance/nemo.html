FINDING NEMO [2023]

I led, choreographed, and staged this piece as part of my dance team's annual show, Mocha Show: Mo'Flix 2023. This dance, to Childish Gambino's Stand Tall, is inspired by the calmness and group dynamics of underwater creatures, while simultaneously portraying the emotions of breaking out of one's comfort zone and, well, standing tall.

This piece derives movements from hip hop, house, and contemporary dance styles. Special thanks to my dancers for portraying this piece beautifully.
