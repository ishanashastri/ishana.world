Pangea
9/2019
Last year at HackMIT, my team and I created a web app, Pangea, that makes it
easy for immigrants in the US to find relevant resources, from grocery stores
that sell goods from their home country to local community centers to legal
services and other immigration resources, all equipped with a Telegram translation chatbot. HTML, CSS, JS, Google Maps API, Telegram API
Front Office Sign-in Software
3/2017-6/2017
As Project Manager and Lead Programmer of my team of four, I developed a sign in software
for my high school's front office. It is equipped with the ability to log students who sign in/out
of school at abnormal times while storing their reason and the location of their excuse note,
to enter an admin mode where one can edit the log and enable different settings, and to automatically
email the attendance secretary a CSV of the attendance log at the end of the day. Java, JavaFX, CSS
